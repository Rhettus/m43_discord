## Overview

The Olympus M.Zuiko 25mm f/1.8 is a fast standard prime lens that provides a 50mm equivalent field of view. It's compact, lightweight, and delivers excellent sharpness across the frame.

## Key Features

- Fast f/1.8 aperture for shallow depth of field
- Excellent build quality with metal construction
- Smooth manual focus clutch mechanism
- Fast and silent autofocus
- Great for portraits, street, and low-light photography

## Pros

- Sharp even wide open
- Compact and well-balanced
- Fast autofocus
- Affordable for the quality

## Cons

- Some chromatic aberration at f/1.8
- No weather sealing
- Moderate vignetting at wide apertures
