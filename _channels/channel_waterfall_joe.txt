## About the Channel

Joe Howard is a landscape photographer and avid traveler from Connecticut who launched his YouTube channel @WaterfallJoe in August 2023. As his name suggests, Joe specializes in waterfall photography, combining his passion for discovering these natural wonders with sharing photography techniques and gear recommendations.

## Content Style

- **Waterfall-focused** landscape photography and videography
- **Travel adventures** exploring waterfalls across different regions
- **Photography gear reviews** tailored to landscape and waterfall shooting
- **Practical tutorials** for capturing waterfalls in various conditions
- **Location guides** helping viewers find and photograph waterfalls
