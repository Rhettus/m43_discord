## About the Channel

Red35 Photography is the YouTube channel founded by Jimmy Cheng, an award-winning photographer, filmmaker, and content creator with over 16 years in the industry, and professional videographer/director Tracey Fung. Based in Hong Kong and now the UK, Jimmy is an OM System (formerly Olympus) Ambassador, educator, and speaker who supports entrepreneurs within the photographic and filmmaking industry as well as artists and creatives.

## Content Style

- **Informal and accessible** - avoiding jargon-based technical reviews
- **Real-world experience** - accumulated knowledge from professional environments
- **Gear reviews** focusing on affordable and interesting equipment
- **Photography vlogs** showing everyday photography life
- **Comparison videos** ("Battle of..." series) comparing similar lenses/cameras
- **Fun tips and techniques** for improving skills using Olympus/OM System
- **Collaborative content** featuring other photographers and creatives

## Why We Recommend This Channel

Jimmy and Tracey's approach is refreshingly informal and practical. Rather than diving deep into technical specifications, they focus on how gear performs in real-world shooting situations. Their "Battle of..." comparison series is particularly helpful for photographers deciding between similar lenses or cameras. Jimmy's background as an LRPS (Licentiate of the Royal Photographic Society) award winner and professional photographer brings credibility, while his informal presentation style keeps content accessible and enjoyable.

## Best For

- OM System/Olympus users wanting practical gear advice
- Those interested in street and travel photography
- Photographers seeking honest, real-world reviews
- Learning from professional photographers' experiences
- Anyone interested in the business side of photography
- Understanding lens choices through direct comparisons

## Photography Background

**Early Influences**: Jimmy's photography journey began with a point-and-shoot camera his parents gave him for travel. Friends in university initially sparked his interest with amazing pictures, and as he grew more experienced, he was heavily influenced by masters like Steve McCurry and Henri Cartier-Bresson.

**Professional Work**: With over 16 years in the industry, Jimmy works professionally in multiple genres:
- Surprise proposals
