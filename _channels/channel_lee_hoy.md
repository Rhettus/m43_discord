## About the Channel

Lee Hoy is a seasoned wildlife and nature photographer based in the Davis Mountains of West Texas, living partially off-grid in his 18-acre mountain paradise. He's an OM System Ambassador and serves as Senior Photography Workshop Instructor for Wildside Nature Tours and Precision Camera & Video. After 30+ years of lugging heavy DSLRs, he discovered M43 and never looked back.

## Content Style

- **Educational and inspirational** workshop-level teaching
- **Field craft emphasis** - teaching where and when, not just how
- **Multiple genres** - wildlife, landscape, macro, night sky
- **Honest and authentic** approach to photography education
- **Practical wisdom** from decades of professional experience

## Why We Recommend This Channel

Lee's passion for teaching shines through every video. He emphasizes practice, learning from failure, and mastering field craft alongside technical skills. His understanding of animal behavior and ecology, combined with his photographic expertise, makes his content uniquely valuable. He believes in hard work, authenticity, and helping others improve their skills.

## Best For

- Wildlife photographers wanting to improve field craft
- Night sky and astrophotography enthusiasts
- OM System camera users
- Those interested in photography workshops
- Learning animal behavior and ecology for better photos

## Key Philosophy

"Always be open to receiving feedback on any image and don't get disappointed your images don't look like those from someone else. Develop your own photographic vision and style. Never stop learning your craft!"
