## About the Channel

Created by Emily May Lowery, Micro Four Nerds started in March 2017 with the humble goal of reaching 100 subscribers within a year. The channel has grown into one of the largest independent M43 communities, spawning a thriving Facebook group with over 4,000 members. Emily is a self-taught photographer with a background in music and sound design who transitioned into visual content creation.

## Content Style

- **Enthusiastic and approachable** presentation style
- **Tiny Camera Series** proving you can get great results on any budget
- **Video-focused** content with strong emphasis on hybrid shooting
- **Honest gear reviews** covering both Olympus and Panasonic
- **Community-driven** with active engagement

## Why We Recommend This Channel

Emily's infectious enthusiasm and belief that "with the right knowledge you can get amazing photos, regardless of your budget" makes her channel perfect for beginners and enthusiasts alike. Her professional photography experience (12+ years) combined with her educator mindset creates content that's both inspiring and practical.

## Best For

- Video shooters and hybrid photographers
- Those on a budget wanting to maximize their gear
- Panasonic GH series users
- Learning M43 system from the ground up
- Community connection and engagement
