## Four Landscape Photography Tips to Last a Lifetime

Wayne's PetaPixel-featured video shares timeless advice:

1. **Spend time on composition** - If you see a scene you like, get the composition right in-camera before pressing the shutter
2. **Study light quality and direction** - These are not always best at golden hour
3. **Patience pays off** - Don't rush; wait for the right moment
4. **Photograph for yourself** - Capture images that resonate with you personally for true improvement and satisfaction

PetaPixel praised not just the tips but the educational value of watching Wayne's "slow and methodical way he approaches capturing just three scenes over the course of nine hours."
