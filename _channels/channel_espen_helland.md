## About the Channel

Espen Helland is a Norwegian wildlife photographer based in Perthshire, Scotland, with a background in wildlife biology and conservation. After working as an ecologist, he developed a passion for telling the stories of the natural world through photography. Since switching to Olympus (OM System) in 2020, he's become known for his detailed technical knowledge and his popular OM System for Wildlife Photography course, which has sold over 500 courses.

## Content Style

- **Wildlife-focused** with emphasis on birds and mammals

- **Conservation storytelling** combining science and art
- **Field tutorials** showing real-world shooting scenarios

## Why We Recommend This Channel

Espen's combination of wildlife biology expertise and photographic skill makes his content uniquely valuable.
