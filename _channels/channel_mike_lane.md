## About the Channel

Mike Lane FRPS (Fellow of the Royal Photographic Society) is a full-time professional wildlife photographer specializing in birds and mammals. Although he travels extensively, his passion is British wildlife - finding more satisfaction photographing wrens and chaffinches than exotic birds in distant rainforests. Mike uses both Panasonic and Olympus M43 cameras, choosing each for different shooting scenarios.

## Why We Recommend This Channel

## Photography Philosophy

Mike is a "great sitter" who spends more time in bird hides than perhaps anyone else. He likens it to fishing - quiet, patient, and deeply relaxing. His approach prioritizes observation and understanding wildlife behavior over gear specs.
