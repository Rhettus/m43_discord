## About the Channel

Jamie Spensley is an award-winning photographer from Birmingham, UK, best known for his detailed, colorful macro photography of bugs. At just 17 years old during the 2020 lockdown, Jamie discovered macro photography as a way to cure boredom in his back garden. Within months, he secured first place in the prestigious Luminar Young Bug Photographer of the Year 2020 competition.

## Content Style

- **Macro insect photography** with incredible detail
- **Focus stacking tutorials** showing advanced techniques
- **Street and landscape** photography with a cinematic aesthetic
- **Behind-the-scenes** process videos
- **Handheld techniques** proving you don't always need a tripod

## Why We Recommend This Channel

Jamie's journey from teenage hobbyist to award-winning photographer in just months is inspiring. His technical mastery of focus stacking combined with his artistic vision creates images that help people overcome their fear of bugs and appreciate their beauty. His use of Olympus M43 gear for handheld macro work demonstrates what's possible with the system.

## Photography Specialties

**Macro Bug Photography**: "A lot of people fear bugs and I shoot in this style to try and help people get over that fear and appreciate the beauty of bugs."

**Focus Stacking**: Jamie achieves incredible detail using focus stacking techniques, shooting multiple images at different focus points and combining them in post-processing.

**Street Photography**: Jamie's street work has a dark, cold, and gritty aesthetic reflecting his feelings towards urban life, drawing inspiration from cinematography in films.

**Product Photography**: The techniques learned in macro photography translate perfectly to small product work like rings and watches.

## Creative Philosophy

"I find satisfaction in capturing ordinary subjects and using my creative skills to make them appear different to how we typically view them."

Jamie shoots multiple genres to keep his creative mind fresh, drawing different inspiration from each style of photography.
